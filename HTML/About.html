About Us


The Eek factor was founded in 2005 by a woman by the name of Chloe Almazien this business has been something that her team and herself has been working on. she has built her skills of rug making over the years and at first pursued it has a hobby, before long she decided to share this hobby by investing her time in creating an establishment that would allow her to teach the craft.


The Eek!!!!Factor is the mind child of a creative bunch of individuals who understands how art impacts the world and by employing people to demonstrate their skills while teaching others we plan to use this company as a source of hope for youth and anybody out there with a creative spark who has yet to reach their heights.


The money that is made in by this company doesnt just grow the business, it is also used to to donate to shelters and orphanages all across the western cape and our goal is to reach all of southern africa. Not only will this be a self investment on your part as your customer ,but youll be similtaniously be helping those in need as well.
